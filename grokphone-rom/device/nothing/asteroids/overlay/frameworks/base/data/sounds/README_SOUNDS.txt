# Custom GrokPhone Sounds

Place your own .ogg sound files here to replace stock UI sounds:

- camera_click.ogg
- camera_focus.ogg
- charging_started.ogg
- dock.ogg
- effect_tick.ogg
- in_call_notification.ogg
- KeypressDelete.ogg
- KeypressInvalid.ogg
- KeypressReturn.ogg
- KeypressSpacebar.ogg
- KeypressStandard.ogg
- Lock.ogg
- LowBattery.ogg
- NFCFailure.ogg
- NFCInitiated.ogg
- NFCTransferComplete.ogg
- NFCTransferInitiated.ogg
- Trusted.ogg
- Unlock.ogg
- VideoRecord.ogg
- VideoStop.ogg
- WirelessChargingStarted.ogg

Recommended style: Calm, minimal, high-quality, covenant-aligned sounds (avoid aggressive or childish tones).

After adding files, they will be included via the overlay system.
