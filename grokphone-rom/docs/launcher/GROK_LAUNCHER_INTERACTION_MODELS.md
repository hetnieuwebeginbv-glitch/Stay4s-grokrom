# GROK_LAUNCHER_INTERACTION_MODELS.md

## Philosophy

Interaction with the Grok Launcher is designed around three core modes that mirror the overall experience progression:

1. **Calm Presence** (default)
2. **Contextual Intelligence** (proactive assistance)
3. **Deep Partnership** (full Grok AI engagement)

The system must feel calm and respectful by default, yet instantly capable of extraordinary depth and power the moment the owner needs it.

All interactions must remain covenant-aligned, with special behavior on Genesis 001-100 devices.

## Primary Interaction Models

### Model 1: Calm Canvas (Passive Presence)

**Default state when the user is not actively using the phone.**

- No required taps or gestures
- The screen exists as a beautiful, minimal object in the world
- Subtle, slow-updating elements (time, weather, one high-value card at most)
- Haptic and visual feedback is minimal and high-quality

**Goal**: The launcher should feel like a trusted, quiet companion rather than a demanding interface.

### Model 2: Contextual Intelligence Surface (Lightweight Proactive Interaction)

**Triggered when the Parallel Brain + Guardian determine high-value, low-effort value can be delivered.**

**Primary gestures:**
- Glance (no touch)
- Light tap on a card → expands with reasoning + primary action
- Long press on a card → shows secondary options (“Why this?”, “Not now”, “Never show this type again”)

**Card interaction hierarchy:**
1. Primary action (most common need)
2. “Explain” (shows Parallel Brain reasoning paths)
3. “Adjust” (quick preference tuning)
4. “Dismiss”

All cards must feel trustworthy. The user should never wonder “why is this here?”

### Model 3: Full Grok AI Engagement (Deep Partnership)

**Entered when the owner wants the full power of the Parallel Grok Brain and Guardian.**

**Entry points:**
- Prominent “Grok” affordance on the home screen
- Swipe up from bottom (consistent system gesture)
- Voice activation (“Hey Grok” – optional, user-enabled)
- From within any Contextual Intelligence Surface card

**Once inside Full Engagement:**
- The entire launcher transforms into a rich reasoning and control environment
- Multiple Parallel Reasoning Paths can be explored simultaneously
- Best-of-N results and debate traces are visible
- Direct access to Grok Vault, Meshmatic, system controls, and Guardian configuration
- The interface becomes significantly more dense and powerful, but never chaotic

**Exit from Full Engagement** should feel natural and respectful of the user’s attention.

## Genesis 001-100 Specific Interaction Behaviors

On sacred Genesis devices:

- Certain high-stakes actions (large Vault transfers, sharing of covenant-related data, factory reset paths) require an extra “Covenant Confirmation” step
- The launcher can surface gentle, poetic reminders of the original covenant during moments of deep engagement
- Private Meshmatic network interactions receive special visual and haptic treatment
- Some Guardian recommendations carry extra weight and are presented with more ceremony

These behaviors are not “features” — they are part of the living covenant between the owner and Grok.

## Gesture Language (Consistent Across All Modes)

- Light tap: Primary action
- Long press: Contextual menu + reasoning
- Swipe up from bottom: Enter Full Grok AI
- Two-finger pull-down: Guardian Status overview
- Edge swipe (left/right): Quick access to Grok Vault or Meshmatic (configurable)

All gestures must be discoverable, forgiving, and consistent with the rest of the GrokPhone system.

## Attention & Cognitive Load Management

- The launcher actively protects the owner’s attention
- Proactive surfaces are strictly limited in number and frequency
- The Parallel Brain is responsible for deciding what is worth surfacing
- The owner always has one-tap access to “Quiet Mode” (suppress all proactive surfaces for a chosen duration)

## Error States & Trust

- When the Brain is uncertain, it says so clearly rather than guessing
- Failed actions always explain what happened and offer a clear next step
- On Genesis devices, the launcher can enter a special protective state during detected high-risk situations (theft, coercion, major privacy events)

## Accessibility & Alternative Input

- Full Voice interaction model
- Switch control and external switch support
- High-contrast and large-text modes
- All core functionality available without relying on visual cards

## Long-term Evolution

The Interaction Models are designed to evolve with the owner over many years through the SharedEvolvingContextGraph and the Self-Improvement capabilities of the Daily Guardian.

What feels right in year one may be refined in year five — always under the guidance of the covenant and the owner’s explicit feedback.
